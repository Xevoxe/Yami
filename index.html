The Kinfallen

It has been a millennia since the magical source of power known as Yami was first harnessed by the Chosen. With nothing else to rival their powerful Yami magic, the known world was forever changed as the Chosen united the continent under one empire, The Great Yami Empire. However, Yami is a living life force and would not accept just anyone.

Overtime a divergence between the Chosen and the people occurred resulting in the people forcefully taking the Yami power into themselves. By doing so these individuals will pay a terrible price as a corruption forms turning them insane and destructive. Now strict laws govern the use of Yami as the dark times of the Forlorn Realm remain a subtle reminder of how close the people came to destruction.

Our team is made up of skilled artists, programmers, writers, and musicians that span the globe. Each person bringing an array of talents and skills to create fun and immersive worlds.

Videogames and design is our passion and we want to share it with you.
